MASTER SCAN

Build a powerful AI feature called Master Scan.

Master Scan allows users to drag and select any area of the screen. After capture, AI automatically analyzes the selected content and provides the most relevant actions inside a single unified workspace.

There should be NO separate pages or sub-menus for each capability. Everything should work from one intelligent interface.

---

USER FLOW

1. User clicks "Master Scan".

2. A screen selection overlay appears.

3. User drags a rectangle around any content.

4. Capture the selected region.

5. Automatically detect the content type.

Supported content:

- Text
- Images
- Articles
- Blogs
- Research Papers
- PDFs
- Study Notes
- Screenshots
- Math Equations
- Job Descriptions
- Infographics

6. Display relevant actions and AI outputs inside the same workspace.

---

OCR & TEXT EXTRACTION

If an image contains text:

- Extract all text accurately.
- Preserve formatting when possible.
- One-click copy button.
- Export extracted text.

---

MATH SOLVER

If a mathematical equation is detected:

- Automatically recognize the equation.
- Integrate with WolframAlpha API or an advanced reasoning model.
- Solve step-by-step.
- Explain each step clearly.
- Show final answer.
- Support Algebra, Calculus, Statistics, Geometry and Trigonometry.

---

ARTICLE / BLOG ANALYSIS

If an article, webpage, blog or document is detected:

Generate:

- 30-Second Summary
- Key Takeaways
- Important Facts
- Main Insights
- Final Conclusion

---

RESEARCH PAPER ANALYSIS

If a research paper is detected:

Generate:

- Plain-English Explanation
- Research Objective
- Methodology
- Key Findings
- Limitations
- Practical Applications
- Important Facts
- 30-Second Summary

Make technical content easy to understand.

---

SMART PLANNER

Master Scan includes an AI planner called Smarty.

Users can create:

- Study Plans
- Interview Preparation Plans
- Learning Roadmaps
- Project Plans
- Career Plans

Smarty should ask intelligent follow-up questions before generating a plan.

Examples:

Study Planner:

- What exam are you preparing for?
- Target score?
- Hours available per day?
- Exam date?

Interview Planner:

- Company name?
- Role?
- Experience level?
- Interview date?

Based on answers, generate highly personalized and realistic plans with milestones, timelines and daily goals.

- Extract Key Insights
- Analyze Content

AI should automatically adapt output based on the detected content.

Master Scan should feel like an intelligent AI copilot that can understand anything visible on a user's screen and instantly provide analysis, explanations, planning, learning assistance and actionable insights from a single interface.
