Build the "Truth Layer" feature for my Chrome extension.

Core Idea:

Users should not copy links or search manually.

Instead, the extension should let users capture a product directly from any e-commerce website (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.) and instantly reveal what people genuinely think about that exact product across the internet.

--------------------------------------------------
USER FLOW
--------------------------------------------------

1. User clicks "Truth Layer".

2. A screen-selection mode activates.

3. The page darkens with a transparent overlay.

4. User drags a rectangle around a product card or product section.

5. Capture the selected area.

6. Extract:
   - Product Name
   - Brand
   - Price
   - Rating
   - Product Image
   - Model Number (if available)

7. Identify the exact product.

8. Automatically search and analyze the SAME PRODUCT across multiple sources.

--------------------------------------------------
MULTI-PLATFORM ANALYSIS
--------------------------------------------------

Analyze the product from:

1. Customer Reviews
   - Amazon
   - Flipkart
   - Other marketplace reviews

2. Reddit
   Search discussions about:
   - product review
   - product issues
   - product experience
   - product comparison

3. Quora
   Search:
   - Is this product worth buying?
   - Long-term user experiences
   - Recommendations

4. Google Search
   Search:
   - Professional reviews
   - Expert opinions
   - Product comparisons
   - Reliability reports

5. YouTube Reviews
   Analyze:
   - Review titles
   - Review summaries
   - Frequently mentioned strengths
   - Frequently mentioned weaknesses

--------------------------------------------------
AI INTELLIGENCE LAYER
--------------------------------------------------

The AI must combine all findings and remove marketing noise.

Detect:

- Common praise
- Common complaints
- Reliability concerns
- Hidden issues
- Value for money
- Long-term ownership experience
- Community sentiment
- Expert sentiment

Do not simply summarize reviews.

Find patterns repeated across platforms.

If a complaint appears repeatedly on Amazon, Reddit and YouTube, treat it as highly reliable.

If praise appears only in marketing-style reviews, reduce confidence.

--------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT
--------------------------------------------------

Display a premium dashboard.

--------------------------------------------------
TRUTH SCORE
--------------------------------------------------

Score: XX/100

Meaning:

90-100 = Excellent
80-89 = Recommended
70-79 = Good
60-69 = Average
Below 60 = Avoid

--------------------------------------------------
TRUTH SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------

A concise AI-generated summary explaining the reality of the product.

Example:

"Although the product maintains a high marketplace rating, users across Reddit and long-term reviews consistently report battery degradation after several months. Overall performance remains strong, but durability concerns reduce the final score."
